About Us

The Rook Platoon is an interstellar fraternity of Miners, Traders and Anti-Xeno Hunters founded in October of 3306. Supported by commanders of all ranks and creeds, Rook Platoon molds fledgling commanders into masters of their trades.

Mission Statement:
"To support squadron based operations in a low pressure, welcoming environment. To nurture a fun filled, casual learning environment To assist every member of the squadron in an array of tricks, pilot trade tips and engage in multiple activities throughout the galaxy in an efficient and effective manner"
