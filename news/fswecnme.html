First Squadron Wide Event: A Mini Expedition to the California Nebula!

To initiate many more squadron wide events to come, the Landstrider Legion has announced a mini-expedition to the California Nebula, in celebration of the squadron's creation!

Led by CMDR Sirius T. Dog, the fleet carrier Spaceship Neptune (V5K-TKG) will depart V371 Normae at 9:00a.m CST on Sunday, September 5, 2021 (2:00p.m UTC on the same date). A departure from the California Nebula will occur at 8:00p.m CST on the same day (1:00a.m UTC on Monday, September 6, 2021), however this time is flexible based on the wishes of attendees.
Updates to the expedition and additional information will be posted here as necessary!
