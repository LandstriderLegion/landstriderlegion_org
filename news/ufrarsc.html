Update from CMDR Rex Ardem Regarding the Creation of the Landstrider Legion

Following the creation of the Landstrider Legion, squadron leader CMDR Rex Ardem has said the following:

o7 everyone, and hello Commanders.
It is my great honor to formally welcome you all to the Landstrider Legion.
We hope to provide some clarification and updates with regard to the status of the squadron.
As some of you are aware, the Landstrider Legion is a squadron established in response to the Legion’s leadership divesting ourselves from our previous organization, the Rook Platoon, due to leadership and playstyle differences.
Despite our split, it is the stance of the Landstrider Legion that the two squadrons shall remain neutral with each other, as agreed upon by the leadership of both squadrons. In following with such, we wish peace to all who wish the same, and a high-dose Copium prescription to those who may wish ills or otherwise.
Furthermore, while the overall state of the game leaves much to be desired, we believe there is still plenty of fun to be had out in the black. To such ends, I am happy to report that as we have become of requisite size for a Player-Made Faction, our BGS Operations will be underway in the coming days, and more details will be relayed as they are finalized.
Currently, the Legion asks of all commanders to please:

  • Read [our] Terms of Service in [the rules and regulations channel on our Discord]
  • Request membership in our In-game and Inara squadron [Landstrider Legion | L4ND]
  • Move all in game assets to our Rally Point system V371 Normae


Furthermore, any commanders wishing to establish friendly or diplomatic relations may request such permissions from the Order of Imperators at the [on our Discord]
Lastly, If there are further questions, please direct them towards [general chat on our Discord] that all inquiries may be accounted and answered in once location.
We thank you for your support for this in endeavor, and we’ll see you out in the black.
For the Honor of the Legion,
The Order of Imperators
